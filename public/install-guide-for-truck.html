For iPhone (Apple)

  • 4 Tap “Add” in the top right.
The app will now appear on your home screen like a regular app.

For Android

  • 1 Open Chrome, scan the QR code or go to the link.
  • 2 Tap the 3-dot menu (top-right).
  • 3 Tap “Add to Home screen” or “Install App”.
  • 4 Tap “Install” when prompted.
The app icon will now be available on your phone’s home screen.
